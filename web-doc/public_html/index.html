Getting Started

DIA (Digital Irrigation Automation) is project started with Yarl it Hub with a mission of Automate irrigation system. This project is To develop the irrigation system to save water to the next generation. Water is an important source for life and now it’s becoming polluted and Unavailable. This means in few years’ water could be a difficult thing to get. Is south Asian countries irrigations is an open irrigation, so the Percentage of water wastage is high.

Our aim is to reduce the wastage of water in irrigation. We introduce weather forecast for the irrigation. If It could be rain today so we don’t need to irrigate. We also introduce moisture sensors to find out water level on the land so it will tell you if the Plant need water or it will warn you there are too much water.
